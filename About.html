Sarah Fassy

Education
Sarah majored in hospitality education by earning her undergraduate degree from the Hospitality and Tourism School Of Saint Quentin en Yvelines and by holding a postgraduate degree from ESSEC Business School where she earned a Master of Business Administration Major in Hospitality Management.
She is bi-lingual (French and English) and has a basic understanding of Spanish.

Experience
Sarah has over 10 years of experience in the industry. Prior to joining MKG Group last year, Sarah started her career in 2006 with Accor Group, where she gained experience in F&B operations.
In 2010, she attended a management training in India with Oriental Cuisines Pvt Ltd Group, where she was in charge of the repositioning strategy of “The French Loaf” brand. 
After she earned her undergraduate degree in 2011, she then moved to Australia to work with Merivale, owning group of a growing portfolio of more than 65 restaurants, bars, pubs, hotels and function spaces in Sydney.
Since 2012, she is based in Paris and worked with the Paris Inn Group as an assistant project manager in 2013 while completing at the same time her MBA at ESSEC.

Major highlights in her career include the repositioning and/or rebranding of several 4- and 5-star hotels in Paris, where she performed hotels' audit, feasibility analyses, pre-diagnosis simulations and project management activities.

Current Responsibilities
Sarah joined MKG Group in 2016, as a senior research analyst. She is in charge of OlaKala, a survey management platform destined for hospitality and tourism actors.
